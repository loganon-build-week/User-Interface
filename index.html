What is LogAnon?

LogAnon is a product that allows you to anonymously login and use services that require email verification like Yelp, news sites, promotions that require e-mail sign up, and more. LogAnon will create unique email logins for each service you use and manage them separately for you so that you can seamlessly use all the services you like, while feeling secure that you are minimizing your digital footprint and protecting your privacy.



-------------------- Features --------------------

Masks your digital footprint and maintains anonymity

Keeps all of your fake accounts in one place

Detects and activates confirmation e-mails/codes.
