What is LogAnon?

LogAnon is a product that allows you to anonymously login and use services that require email verification like Yelp, news sites, promotions that require e-mail sign-up, and more. LogAnon will create unique email logins for each service you use and manage them separately for you so that you can seamlessly use all the services you like, while feeling secure that you are minimizing your digital footprint and protecting your privacy.

Why Fake My Info?

Most people today make great use of the internet and its many applications. On a daily basis we go from site to site, and in our wake, we leave a very telling digital footprint. LogAnon helps users avoid unnecessary data collection by any who may seek it.

Data is the gold of the modern era, and each time you absent-mindedly enter an e-mail, username, or password, you freely disperse this wealth of info to... someone? somewhere? We might do this to dismiss a pop-up, or gain access to a site functionality that requires 'a profile' to continue, but make no mistake, your data has been collected and stored for future use.



A Thought Experiment...

Let's take a look at the value of a simple sign-up form to read that article you were interested in. With your e-mail, this site and its affiliates can now directly reach and market to you until the end of time. Maybe the password you used to sign-up for that article is the same password you use for something much more important? Were there security questions? A field for a zip code? Great, now this site has got a rough outline of your identity, and location too.

Now consider the number of different sites you may have similarly signed in to, this has even wider implications. Someone, or some algorithm, with the knowledge of every site you've visited or signed up for, would have a rather precise idea of your likes, dislikes, and needs. Selling to you has just become so much easier, expect targeted ads and prices that reflect your desires.
